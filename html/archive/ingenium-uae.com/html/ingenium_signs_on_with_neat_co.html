February - 2006.
In our ongoing quest to diversify our business model, Ingenium Partners has signed up to handle all accounting and IT work for US based Neat Consultants Inc. The New York registered firm is a retailer of convenience goods and household items in the Dallas/ Ft. Worth, Texas Area.

Neat Consultants Inc. has been in business since 1997, and has owned a variety of business models. The client represents the diversity in culture and abilities of Ingenium Partners FZE, since they have taken on to handle all accounting aspects of the retailer, from basic functions like book-keeping to the filing of corporate tax returns.

In the field of IT, Ingenium Partners has been successfully able to build and deploy a reliable network model for Neat Consultants Inc. Since the deployment of the network, the company has seen negligible problems with their network. Ingenium Partners has also assisted Neat Consultants Inc, in setting up a retail Point of Sale (POS) system, which has made management of inventory and generation of management reports a much simpler process.

Ingenium Partners FZE, a UAE and US based financial management and IT Consulting firm, is one of the fastest growing business consultants providing services to businesses globally.
