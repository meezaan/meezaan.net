What’s New at Ingenium Partners FZE

The Board of Directors
Ingenium Partners FZE is expanding at a growing rate with several new nominations to the Board of Directors and Management Team. See who’s joining the future of the Middle East Consulting and Outsourcing Business here, and check back as our team of advisors and professionals multiplies further.

Ingenium signs on with Neat Consultants Inc.
In our ongoing quest to diversify our business model, Ingenium Partners has signed up to handle all accounting and IT work for US based Neat Consultants Inc. The New York registered firm is a retailer of convenience goods and household items in the Dallas/ Ft. Worth, Texas Area.

Ingenium takes possession of new office in Sharjah
Ingenium Partners FZE has moved to a new location at Hamriyah Free Zone, Sharjah. The new facility provides Ingenium Partners with a central location to manage the operations in Asia, Europe, and the United States.

Ingenium Partners signs deal with InnoMakings, Inc.
Ingenium Partners FZE has signed a deal with InnoMakings, Inc., a US based information technology consulting firm. This partnership adds more credit to the name of, and adds to the extensive network of consultants at Ingenium Partners.

New Web Site Launched
Ingenium Partners FZE, one of the largest international accounting and IT consulting groups, has launched it’s new web site. It is still under construction, but work is in progress and all pages will soon be accessible.
