Ingenium expands into Dubai

April - 2006.
With the rapid growth in business, Ingenium Partners has expanded to a new facility in Dubai. The facility provides the company with a central location and easier access to the diversified client base and talent that the city of Dubai houses.

With innovation in business and technology in horizon, the Dubai market is expanding and is in dire need of business and technical consultants to help it. Ingenium Partners has provided a strong support base for Dubai business to depend upon, and has delivered on its Worry Free Guarantee in every instance.

The company has also expanded it’s outsourcing operation, catering to countries not just in the Middle East, but across the Americas and Europe. With the dedication and personal involvement of upper management in all projetcs, Ingenium Partners provides a level of service that redefines professionalism in the Dubai market.
