December - 2005.
Ingenium Partners FZE has signed a deal with InnoMakings, Inc., a US based information technology consulting firm. This partnership adds more credit to the name of, and adds to the extensive network of consultants at Ingenium Partners.

InnoMakings, Inc., which shares part common ownership with Ingenium Partners, has been in the IT consulting business for several years, and operates other IT concerns too. FogBox LLC acts as InnoMakings, Inc.’s emarketing and web design unit, while InnoDomains functions as the company’s web hosting and domain registrar unit.

Along with the support and stability that a stable, established company like InnoMakings, Inc. has to offer to Ingenium Partners, the two companies now share a common network of experts and professionals. This makes for a strong partnership, and between the two companies, some of the world’s most strong resume’s are available to businesses all over the world for consulting.
