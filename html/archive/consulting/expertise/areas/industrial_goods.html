Spanning the industrial sector, companies using traditional competitive models are producing rapidly diminishing returns. Their managers must go beyond the obvious in order to find new sources of profitable growth.

In capital-intensive industries like paper, cement, and metals, companies are searching for alternatives to the never ending fight for global market share and the downward pressure on prices and margins.

And in nearly every industrial segment, the rapid proliferation of business-to-business e-commerce and fast globalization of econmic resources is transforming how products are made, marketed, distributed, and sold—and by whom.

Our Practice

Asif Nawaz Consulting's Industrial Goods practice serves two broad industrial segments: capital-intensive industries and engineered products and services. We help companies in these sectors think beyond the limits of their traditional business models, choose where and how they should add value, and reorganize to defend themselves against new threats and exploit new opportunities.
